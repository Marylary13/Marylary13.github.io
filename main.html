My Journey

Highschool

My academic journey took a significant turn when I joined Bryanston High School in grade 10. I decided to select pure maths, biology, physics, and history for my high school subjects. Prior to this, I had attended a small school with limited access to computers, and I was unaware of the existence of subjects like IT and CAT. Despite my passion for gaming and technology from a young age, I never considered a career in software development.

Matric

My matric year was a challenging time, both academically and personally. Despite my hard work, I faced unexpected setbacks that left me feeling defeated. I did manage to pass all my subjects, although I didn't achieve the marks I needed to get into my desired course. Feeling lost and uncertain, I stumbled upon Belgium Campus ITversity and attended their open day. I was impressed by their Bachelor of Computing course and decided to take a chance and apply. This marked the beginning of my journey into the world of programming, and I was eager to learn and explore the opportunities that lay ahead.

Freshman in University

In my first year of university, I encountered a lot of challenges and often questioned my ability to make it in the field of programming. Despite this, I stayed positive and put a lot of work into my studies. My hard work paid off, as I managed to achieved seven distinctions that year.

The Present

Its been a long and hard journey. Every year got more and more difficult but in the end I managed to get where I wanted to be and found my passions. Third year has been quite the challenge, and yet I'm still standing STRONG. I hope to advance further in my career and studues, I feel like you're never too old to learn and I want to continue gaining knowledge as well as experience.
